Cryptocurrency <> Money

🌏 International

🤑 Transactions

  • Refill phone or buy gift cards: BitRefill

💳 Cards

Not crypto cards

  • PayPal (also offers other kinds of cards)

Crypto cards

  • Crypto.com (card shipping is only available at Singapore, the United States, Asia Pacific and European Union)
  • (privileges Europe) Monolith (no monthly fees and no issuing fee - card shipping is only available at Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia (Provisional membership), Republic of Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, United Kingdom, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Gibraltar, San Marino, Monaco, Guadeloupe, Reunion, Martinique, Isle of Man, Jersey, Guernsey, The Aland Islands, Saint-Martin; unless its list is updated)
  • (privileges Europe) Wirex (monthly fees of $1.50 - card shipping is only available at Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Singapore, Slovakia, Slovenia, United Kingdom, Spain, Sweden; unless its list is updated)

🇪🇺 Europe

🤑 Transactions

  • Pay to any IBAN account: BitBill

🇧🇷 Brazil

🤑 Transactions

  • Pay bills, send remittances to Brazil or Europe, refill your phone with credits or do a lookup for CPF/CNPJ: BitBol
  • Pay bills, refill phone or do bank transfers: KaMoney (they haves their own prepaid card, but with monthly fees of R$5 and a minimum refill of R$10)

💳 Cards

Not crypto cards

  • PagSeguro (offers account card, credit card or prepaid card)

Crypto cards

  • KaMoney (specialized for crypto, but with monthly fees of R$5 and a minimum refill of R$10)

💰 Banking

  • FoxBit (buy cryptocurrencies with money or exchange your cryptocurrencies for money to your bank)

🇦🇺 Australia

🤑 Transactions

  • Pay bills, pay to bank account, buy gift vouchers/cards, and if you have a business also accept crypto: Paid by Coins
  • Pay to BPAY bill, bank account, credit card, PayID, PayPal or buy gift cards Bit2Bill (small variety of gift cards when compared to Paid by Coins)
  • Pay to BPAY bill, credit card and bank account: Living Room of Satoshi

This list is growing. If you know local services (like I'd added the ones I know in Brazil), you can comment on DeBank's posts at Substack or Publish0x or send a email to daniellmesquita@protonmail.com.
