My diverse creativity is used for creating things that I find missing and can be useful for the humankind.

From a simple music to a complex Linux distro: the different things I create, surprisingly, can have no relating to each other. I can't get focused in a single project for more than 4 days: I move to a new and totally new different one. I used to be better giving ideas to projects than working on them, but nowadays I got more experience thanks to Bash (2019), better learning the languages' syntax logic (2020) and AI (2023). I begin my projects as virtual notes or draws in papers. I used to turn they into reality using a browser to design structure (2014), and the Construct 2 game engine to "program" (2014 - nowadays the GPT-powered CursorIDE helps me building even fully-fledged ReactJS and Flutter applications). I live for creating things, and I'm pleasured because people can be happy and have their lives simplified/securitized by using these stuff.
